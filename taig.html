The Actually Impossible Game

If you click a number, every number to the left of it will be deducted 1 and every number to the right of it will be added 1. Numbers loop around, so -1 changes to 4 and 5 changes to 0. Try to get all five numbers to display 0.



The Not-So-Impossible Variation

If you click a number, every number to the left of it will be deducted 1 and every number to the right of it will be added 1. Numbers loop around, so -1 changes to 9 and 10 changes to 0. Try to get all six numbers to display 0.
