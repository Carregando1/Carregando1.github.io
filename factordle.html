How To Play Factordle

Factordle is a math-based number game where you need to guess a target number between 1000 and 9999 within 8 tries.

Each guess gives you information on the factors of the target number. An example guess is shown below, where the boxes in a blue border indicate the guess, 1250.

1
2
5
0
12
25
50
125
250
1250

Both 2 and 12 are factors of the target number.

At least 1 factor (other than 1) of 50, 250, and 1250 is a factor of the target number.

No factors (other than 1) of 5, 25, and 125 are a factor of the target number.

The number 1 will always be a factor, and the number 0 will never be a factor.

Once your guess matches the target number (when the bottom box is blue), you win.
